About

Curious, hands-on, and focused on building real systems.

My interest in semiconductor manufacturing started during my time at Samsung Austin Semiconductor. Before working there, I understood the importance of semiconductors, but seeing a fabrication facility firsthand gave me a completely different perspective.

I became fascinated by the scale, complexity, and precision required to manufacture chips, as well as the countless systems and tools that make the process possible.

I enjoy hands-on engineering, learning quickly, solving technical problems, and improving through feedback. I am especially interested in the equipment and systems that make semiconductor fabrication possible.
